Demon Slayer
Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba (鬼滅の刃, Kimetsu no Yaiba, or "Blade of Demon Destruction") is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Koyoharu Gotouge. The story is set in early 20th century Taishō-era Japan, where man-eating demons roam the land at night. The story follows Tanjiro Kamado, a 13-year-old boy who sets out to train with the Demon Slayer Corps after his family is murdered and his sister Nezuko is turned into a demon. Tanjiro's goal is to avenge his family's deaths and find a way to turn Nezuko back into a human
Jujutsu Kaisen
Jujutsu Kaisen (呪術廻戦, rgh. "Sorcery Battle") is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Gege Akutami. It has been serialized in Shueisha's shōnen manga magazine Weekly Shōnen Jump since March 2018, with its chapters collected in 27 tankōbon volumes as of July 2024.
Spy x Family
Spy × Family (stylized as SPY×FAMILY and pronounced "spy family") is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Tatsuya Endo. The story follows a spy who has to "build a family" to execute a mission, not realizing that his adopted daughter is a telepath, and the woman he agrees to marry is a skilled assassin.
Chainsaw Man
Chainsaw Man follows the story of Denji, an impoverished teenager who makes a contract that fuses his body with that of a dog-like Devil named Pochita, granting him the ability to transform parts of his body into chainsaws.
Bleach
Bleach follows the adventures of Ichigo Kurosaki, a high school student with the ability to see ghosts, and Rukia Kuchiki, a Shinigami. The early parts of the story focus mainly on the characters. As events unfold, the story begins to delve deeper into the world of these gods of death.
Naruto
Naruto is a Japanese manga series that was adapted into an anime television series that aired in the early 2000s. The story follows Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja from the Hidden Leaf Village who dreams of becoming the village's leader, the Hokage. Naruto is a problem child at the ninja academy and is treated with disdain by the adults in the village due to a secret about his birth
Hell's Paradise
Hell's Paradise: Jigokuraku is an anime series that premiered in April 2023 and is produced by MAPPA. The series is about a group of prisoners and guards who are sent to investigate a mysterious island and become stranded there
My Hero Academia
My Hero Academia ( 僕 ( ぼく ) のヒーローアカデミア, Boku no Hīrō Akademia?) is a manga series serialized by Shonen Jump and written by Kohei Horikoshi. The series has been adapted into an anime series and three movies, as well as spawning the prequel series Vigilantes and spinoffs: School Briefs and Team-Up Missions.
